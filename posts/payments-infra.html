Global payments infrastructure

By The Product Team

Moving money across borders is deceptively difficult. It involves navigating a complex web of correspondent banking networks, local clearing schemes, and regulatory compliance frameworks.

The Speed of Money

Traditionally, international transfers take days. This latency is not a technological limitation but a structural one. We are rebuilding this infrastructure from the ground up to operate at the speed of the internet.

"The financial infrastructure of tomorrow must be as seamless as the information infrastructure of today."

Security at Scale

With great scale comes great responsibility. Securing billions of dollars requires a defense-in-depth approach, utilizing machine learning for fraud detection and hardware security modules for encryption.

We believe that security should not compromise user experience. Our latest update reduces friction for legitimate users while blocking 99.9% of fraudulent attempts.
