The future of fintech is programmable

By The Strategy Team

We are entering a new era of financial technology where money is no longer just a store of value, but a programmable primitive. This shift allows developers to build financial logic directly into their applications.

Key Trends

  • Embedded Finance: Banking services are moving out of banks and into the software you use every day.
  • Real-time Settlement: The expectation of instant liquidity is driving changes in underlying payment rails.
  • Crypto Integration: Blockchain technology is finding its place in cross-border settlements.

What this means for developers

Developers are the new CFOs. By controlling the flow of funds via API, engineering teams can optimize working capital, automate payouts, and create entirely new business models.
